The Hnefatafl design system · v1.0

A system for Hnefatafl

Spare. Classical. A touch archaic. A dual-mode system — paper and dark — rooted in Garamond display, Inter UI, JetBrains Mono for clocks and notation. Hairline rules instead of filled panels. Gradients only on the pieces and the gilded king.

On voice

On color

A paper mode and a dark mode. Warm ivory carries both. Wood only on the board. One accent — capture-red — kept in reserve.

IV

On space & rule

Spacing stays on a tight grid. Radii are almost invisible — 2 to 4 pixels. Only pieces are circular. No pills. No rounded cards.

Spacing

Tight scale

Spacing is measured in single pixels at the low end, rising to 24px. Outside that, prose spacing takes over.

Borders

Three weights

All borders are 1px. Weight is communicated through opacity, not thickness.
  • subtle — row dividers
  • control — chip / input rest
  • strong — selected / focus

V
Section five

The pieces

The pieces are the brand anchor. Ivory, obsidian, and a gilded king. Never flatten them into silhouettes — the physicality is the point.

Anatomy

Three bodies

Defender, attacker, king. Gradients render sphericity; the king's gilding is a conic blur behind a ringed glyph.
  • Border 1.5–2px, piece-border token
  • Drop shadow at 2/2/3, 25% black
  • King gilding = conic-gradient of warm ramp

VI
Section six

On components

Hairline bordered rectangles. Selected states flip to an ink fill. No gradients, no shadows, no hover lifts — the book stays flat.

Wordmark

Hnefatafl

Garamond italic with the stressed second syllable in roman. The mark carries both modes unchanged.
Hnefatafl
Buttons

Three weights

Outlined primary flips ink on hover. Filled for irreversible actions. Text buttons carry their own underline.
  • Padding 10 · 18, uppercase 0.16em
  • Never more than one filled button per surface
Chips

For flat option sets

Chips are for 3–6 mutually-exclusive options. Selection flips the border to full ink.
  • Use for side · difficulty · time
  • Never use a dropdown instead
Attackers Defenders Random
Easy Medium Hard
Untimed 5 min 10 min 15 min
Input

Underlined field

Inputs are Garamond, to match body prose. The underline is a hairline that darkens on focus. No filled fields.
Entry card

Action rectangles

The entry card is the workhorse of the home page. Hairline border, Garamond label, ink-washed action row at the foot.

On patterns

Three recurring layouts: the centered hero, the modal with field rows, the ledger. Each is built from the primitives above.

Hero

Centered invocation

Eyebrow, Garamond display with italic stress, hairline rule with a centered dot, italic lede.
  • Dot-rule width ≈ 120px, centered
  • Gilded accent reserved for one italic run

Modal

Field rows

Header with eyebrow + serif title + italic sub. Each body row is label · control, separated by hairlines. Actions are right-aligned, hairline-topped.
  • Label column 92px, uppercase tracking 0.18
  • Never center a modal title
